How do I freeze a card?

Open the card from your Cards page, then tap Freeze. Transactions are blocked immediately. Unfreeze the same way.

Admins can freeze any card in the organisation. Cardholders can freeze their own.

In-flight authorisations complete. New attempts are declined until you unfreeze.
